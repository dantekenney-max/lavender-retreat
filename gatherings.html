The Concept

Any Gathering.
One Setting.

Not every gathering fits a category. Some are corporate. Some are personal. Some are somewhere in between. Blackfoot Lavender Retreat welcomes all of it — because the property itself adapts to whatever energy the group brings.

Whether you're hosting 10 executives around a firepit or 50 guests for a private celebration, the land, the architecture, and the atmosphere do something that a hotel ballroom never can: they make the gathering feel like something.
