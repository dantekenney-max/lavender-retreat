The Land

A Setting That
Does the Work

Blackfoot Lavender Retreat sits on intentionally cultivated land in Blackfoot, Idaho. The property was built from the ground up with one idea at its core: create a place where beauty is inescapable.

Rows of lavender stretch across the fields. A modern glass and wood barn anchors the property. A fountain creates a naturally elegant gathering point. Fire pits glow at dusk. And across it all, Idaho’s wide sky frames everything in gold.

What’s Here

Everything That
Makes It Special

Lavender
Fields

Rows of lavender in full bloom all season long. Chemical-free, naturally fertilized, and cut only when the plants require it. The scent, the color, the texture — nothing like it.

Glass & Wood
Barn

Our signature structure blends modern architecture with natural materials. Floor-to-ceiling glass. Warm wood. A building that feels luxurious without feeling pretentious.

Fountain
Area

A classically elegant fountain creates a natural focal point for ceremonies, portraits, and quiet moments. Beautiful by morning light, magical after dark.

Fire Pit
Gathering Space

Our fire pit area is where evenings come alive. Seating arranged around the flame, open sky above, lavender nearby — the perfect setting for celebrations and conversation.

Pool &
Grounds

The grounds include a stunning pool area and manicured outdoor spaces that extend the property’s natural beauty into every usable corner.

Glamping
Sites

Strategically positioned across the property to maximize views and privacy, our glamping setups let guests sleep within the landscape itself — not just near it.

The Philosophy

Chemical-Free.
Naturally Extraordinary.

We use only natural fertilizers and organic practices across the entire property. No synthetic chemicals, no pesticides, no artificial shortcuts. The lavender blooms as it was meant to — fully and freely.

This isn’t just an environmental choice. It’s a reflection of the property’s character. Authentic. Unhurried. Better for it.
