The Setting

A Place Built
for Two

Blackfoot Lavender Retreat offers an intimate, naturally beautiful setting for couples who want their experience to feel as unique as their love story. Set against sweeping lavender fields, modern glass and wood barn architecture, and Idaho's wide open sky — this is romance at its most real.

Whether you're planning an intimate wedding, a honeymoon escape, an anniversary celebration, or a proposal moment you'll never forget — the property provides a backdrop that simply does the work for you. The lavender, the fountain, the firepit, the golden light — every element creates something genuinely stunning.

The Atmosphere

Surrounded by
Lavender & Light

Our ceremony and gathering spaces are designed to let the property speak for itself. The lavender fields provide a natural canopy of purple during bloom season. The fountain area creates a romantic, classically elegant backdrop. Outdoor seating areas flow naturally into the landscape.

Every space has been thoughtfully positioned to capture the best light, the most scenic angles, and the most intimate atmosphere possible — for weddings, vow renewals, proposals, anniversary dinners, and private evenings under Idaho stars.
