The Experience

Above Expectations,
Under the Stars

There is something singular about falling asleep to the scent of lavender with Idaho's sky above you. Our glamping experience is designed to feel like luxury — not compromise. Not camping with a twist. Actual luxury, set outdoors.

Our portable glamping setups are positioned across the property to maximize views, privacy, and connection to the landscape. Each setup is designed to feel curated and intentional — a true retreat within a retreat.
