The Vision

Wake Up to
Beauty Every Day

Blackfoot Lavender Retreat was created with a single, clear intention — to build a very pretty, inviting, warm environment for meaningful gatherings and unforgettable experiences.

This is a place where beauty surrounds you from the moment you arrive. Where the lavender fields greet you at first light. Where the architecture feels like it grew from the land. Where every gathering space was designed to encourage people to slow down, look up, and truly be present.

We believe that the atmosphere shapes the experience. And we built this property to provide an atmosphere unlike anything else in Idaho.

"This retreat was created with intention — a desire to build a very pretty, inviting, warm environment for meaningful gatherings and unforgettable experiences."
The Vision Behind Blackfoot Lavender Retreat
Our Philosophy

A Natural
Sanctuary

We intentionally do not use pesticides, chemicals, or synthetic fertilizers on this property. We rely exclusively on natural Earth techniques — organic practices that enhance the growth of the plants and keep the ground truly chemical-free.

This is not just a business decision — it's a reflection of who we are. We believe that wellness, beauty, and environmental respect matter. The people who visit this property deserve to breathe clean air, walk through fields that were grown without compromise, and experience nature in its most honest form.

When you're here, you're on land that has been cared for intentionally — the same way we hope to care for every experience we host.

What We Stand For

Beauty, Nature,
and Intention

Modern
Elegance

The architecture of this property blends modern glass and barn design with the warmth of natural materials. Luxury that doesn't feel loud — refined and quietly stunning.

Natural
Beauty

Cultivated beauty in every direction. Lavender fields, manicured gardens, a fountain at the center of it all, and Idaho's vast sky above. Nature is the decoration here.

Meaningful
Gatherings

We built this for connection. For conversations that matter. For moments that people carry with them long after they leave. Every space invites people to come together.

Chemical-Free
Commitment

Natural fertilizers only. Organic practices throughout. Because the people here deserve clean land, and the lavender blooms best when grown the right way.

Warm
Atmosphere

We intentionally designed every detail — the lighting, the flow, the textures — to make people feel at ease, welcome, and unhurried the moment they arrive.

Every Day
Extraordinary

The goal was simple: wake up to beauty every day. Every decision made for this property came back to that. Does it make the place more beautiful? Then it belongs here.
