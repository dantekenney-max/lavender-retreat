What It Is

A Circle of
First Access

The Lavender Circle is our inner community — the people who love this property, follow the seasons, and want to experience everything Blackfoot Lavender Retreat has to offer before it’s announced publicly.

As a member, you’ll receive early booking access, seasonal bloom updates, invitations to member-only events, and everything that happens here before anyone else hears about it.

What Circle Members
Receive

Early
Booking Access

Members get first access to book for peak season dates, lavender bloom weekends, and special events before they’re opened to the public.

Bloom Season
Updates

Lavender blooms on its own schedule. Circle members receive real-time updates on peak bloom weeks so you can plan your visit at the most beautiful time.

Member-Only
Events

Seasonal gatherings, acoustic evenings, harvest experiences, and curated events created exclusively for Lavender Circle members.

Opening
Announcements

Be among the first to know when new experiences, new availability windows, and new developments at the property become available.

Preferred
Communication

Members receive personal, direct communication from the retreat — not automated mass emails. The circle stays small and intentional.

Growing
Over Time

As the retreat grows, so do member benefits. Those who join early become part of the foundational community that helped shape what this place becomes.
