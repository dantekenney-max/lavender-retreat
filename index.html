Our Story

Created
With Intention

This retreat was born from a simple desire — to build something beautiful. A place where the light falls perfectly at golden hour, where the lavender sways gently in the breeze, and where people feel welcome the moment they arrive.

Every inch of Blackfoot Lavender Retreat was designed with care. We use only natural fertilizers and organic practices — no synthetic chemicals touch our land. Our lavender blooms all season long, cut only when the plants themselves require it.

Because here, beauty isn't manufactured. It simply grows.

  • Certified chemical-free, all-natural property
  • Lavender in full bloom all season long
  • Modern glass & wood barn architecture
  • Fountain, firepit & gathering areas
  • Designed primarily for adult experiences
  • Peaceful, intentional, and naturally upscale
